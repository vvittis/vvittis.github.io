ABOUT ME

I am a graduate student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the Technical University of Crete. I received my diploma under the supervision of Antonios Deligiannakis.

I am passionate about streaming distributed systems and theoretical machine learning algorithms. Lately, I am working on ensemble learning, concept drift adaptation and graph theory. For more information you can take a look in my CV.

My current research goals are distributed machine learning algorithms, which translates to me as online neural-networks, deep learning and reinforcement learning. Also, I am looking to improve my thesis by making an efficient dynamic-size ensemble.

Vasileios is my official name but everyone calls me Bill :)
